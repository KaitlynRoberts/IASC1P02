Kaitlyn Roberts grew up in a small town in Ontario, Canada called Bowmanville. Roberts is mainly an abstract artist, either exaggerating or understating reality. She wants to show people a different side and different view points of the world through her art. She currently studies Visual Arts at Brock University in St. Catharines, Ontario and plans to further her studies by travelling the world and in teacher's college. She also plans to exhibit nationally and hopefully internationally. Her goal is to be able to share and teach her passion of art with future generations.
